3Tense icons — dropping the ⭐, giving all three real glyphs

Two ways to icon the three tenses, both retiring the star. The app already teaches the past pair as "una vez · one time" vs "muchas veces · many times" — so both triads make that the visual contrast. 3a Cielo is warm and pictographic (sun / planted flag / repeat loop); 3b Línea is a systematic timeline (a point → one dot → many dots, laid out past-to-now). Each tense keeps a distinct hue so they read apart at a glance. I've applied 3a to the tense-picker and the Play chip below it.
