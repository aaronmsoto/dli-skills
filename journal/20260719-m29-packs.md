## What

First M29 (teacher mode) iteration:

- **`#/pack/<setId>`** (renderPack, standard chrome): one print flow per
  group bundling FIVE blank practice sheets (3 core tenses + the 2 M26
  stretch constructions), each with the M5 Nombre/Fecha header and the
  kid-facing tense hint/example — then a "Claves de respuesta" back
  section with the five filled answer-key tables (tap-to-hear on screen
  under the standard audio gates; plain text in print). Blank cells are
  2.4em tall for handwriting. `break-after: page` gives one sheet per
  page; screen-only helpers (help line, print button) hide in print
  along with the standard chrome.
- **Set screen** — a quiet teacher one-liner ("🍎 Para maestros: 🖨️ Pack
  de clase"), deliberately not a card on the kids' grid.
- Deliberate choice: the Nombre/Fecha fields are visible on screen too
  (unlike Estudia's print-only variant) so teachers preview the exact
  sheet they'll print.
