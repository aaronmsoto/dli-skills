# 2026-07-07 — M8: 🧱 Práctica (rebuild the table, unscored)

**Branch:** `loop/20260707-practica` · **Milestone:** M8 (all criteria met)

## What shipped

- **`#/practica/N/tense`** — the Estudia-style table with the active verb's
  column empty and that verb's forms shuffled in a bank of ≥44px tap
  targets. Tap a word, then its person row: correct fills the cell (which
  becomes tap-to-hear per the standard vocalization rules) and speaks
  person + form; incorrect shakes tile + slot and invites a retry — the
  activity cannot be failed. Columns advance verb by verb through all 5;
  completion earns a Lola celebration and links onward (Estudia / otra
  vez / Elige).
- **Unscored, truly:** no `recordResult`, no stars, no badges;
  STARS_PER_SET stays 30; e2e asserts localStorage is byte-identical
  after a full table rebuild.
- **Entry points:** first action link on the study screen (standing rule:
  Estudia links every activity) and an unscored card on the group screen
  (set cards now 6 voiceless / 7 voiced — e2e counts updated, not
  weakened).
- **`buildPracticaBank(verb, tense, persons)`** in js/game.js — one tile
  per person, duplicates kept; placement checks string equality so
  identical forms (imperfect yo/él) are interchangeable. 3 new unit tests
  (multiset equality across 3 groups × 3 tenses, the duplicate-tile case,
  vosotros inclusion).
- **Icon is 🧱, not the 🧩 written in the GOAL entry** — Empareja already
  owns 🧩 and two activities sharing an icon is a signifier collision (the
  kind of thing M10's Norman audit exists to catch). GOAL/CLAUDE references
  updated in this PR.

## Owner decision recorded: payload budget 100 → 120 KB

The app stood at ~95.0 KB of the 100 KB raw-byte budget before this
feature; a tightly-written Práctica costs ~7 KB. Rather than weaken the
gate silently or golf the code unreadable, the owner was asked and chose
**raise to 120 KB** (still ~30 KB gzipped over the wire; leaves honest
headroom for M9/M10). tests/payload.test.mjs, GOAL (M5/M6), SPEC, and
MASCOT updated; the test still hard-fails at the new ceiling and further
raises remain owner-only.
